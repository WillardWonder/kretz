“Print / Save as PDF” opens your browser’s print window — pick Save as PDF to email a printer or mail house, or print directly. Letters print best from a desktop browser.

Owner names, mailing addresses, and acreage come from the Wisconsin Statewide Parcel Map (State Cartographer's Office / DOA) — the most recent tax roll each county submitted. Some counties don't report every field and names can lag recent sales, so expect a little return-to-sender and skim before mailing. Map locations are parcel centroids; forest & MFL flags come from each parcel's reported property class and MFL/FCL value. This is public tax-parcel data for landowner outreach — not a legal survey or a guarantee of timber. Always honor opt-out requests; for any email use, follow CAN-SPAM.
