Stop sequencing uses the Clarke-Wright savings algorithm with 2-opt and Or-opt local search — a capacitated vehicle-routing solver that runs in your browser. With “real road distances” on, the mileage, drive times, and the lines on the map come from the OpenStreetMap road network (OSRM); turn it off for instant straight-line estimates with no internet. The shared OSRM server is free and best for light daily use — self-host OSRM and set its address under “Routing server” for heavy or private use. Each truck’s Google Maps link carries the stops in optimized order (Google may cap very long routes — the printed route sheet always holds the full list).
