KRETZ VOICE SAMPLES — SYNTHETIC TRAINING FILE
=============================================
Not real correspondence. Written for the Session 2 prompting lab so participants
can compare generic AI output against how Kretz actually sounds, and so the
Kretz DNA Assistant has something to work from.

Use with: "Read these samples. Describe the voice in six rules, then rewrite the
draft below to match it."


-------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT SHOWS UP IN THE KRETZ VOICE
-------------------------------------------------------------------
- Plain words. Short sentences. No throat-clearing before the point.
- Says what it does not know, instead of smoothing over it.
- Employee-owned since 1929 comes up as a fact, not a slogan.
- Neutral about whether to harvest. Advice first, sale second.
- Names a person and a next step. Never ends on "please don't hesitate."
- No exclamation points. No "we're excited to." No "reach out."

PAIR 1 — LANDOWNER, FIRST CONTACT
-------------------------------------------------------------------
GENERIC AI VERSION:
  Dear Valued Landowner,
  We are excited to reach out regarding an incredible opportunity to
  maximize the value of your timber assets! Kretz Lumber is a leading
  provider of forestry solutions, and we would love to partner with you
  on your forest management journey. Please don't hesitate to reach out
  at your earliest convenience!

KRETZ VERSION:
  Hello Mr. Bergstrom,
  Your parcel in the Town of Neva came up while we were looking at
  forested land in Langlade County. I'm a forester with Kretz Lumber in
  Antigo.
  I'm not writing to ask you to sell timber. Plenty of stands are better
  left alone for another ten years, and if that's yours I'll tell you so.
  What I can do is walk it with you and give you a straight read on what
  you have, and handle the Managed Forest Law paperwork if you ever go
  that direction.
  We've been employee-owned since 1929, so the person walking your woods
  is an owner here.
  If you want to set up a walk, call me at the number below. If not,
  that's a fine answer and I won't keep writing.

PAIR 2 — CUSTOMER, DELAYED ORDER
-------------------------------------------------------------------
GENERIC AI VERSION:
  Dear Customer,
  We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused. Due
  to unforeseen circumstances beyond our control, your order has been
  slightly delayed. We greatly value your business and appreciate your
  patience during this time. Rest assured we are doing everything
  possible to expedite your shipment!

KRETZ VERSION:
  Hi Dana,
  Your maple order is going to be two days late. The kiln charge it was
  in didn't come down to target moisture, and I'd rather send it right
  than send it Thursday.
  New ship date is Monday the 14th. If that breaks something on your end,
  call me today and we'll figure out what we can pull from another
  charge.
  Sorry for the shuffle.

PAIR 3 — INTERNAL, SAFETY REMINDER
-------------------------------------------------------------------
GENERIC AI VERSION:
  Team,
  Safety is our number one priority! Please remember to always be aware
  of your surroundings and follow all applicable safety protocols at all
  times. Together, we can create a culture of safety excellence!

KRETZ VERSION:
  Crew,
  We had a near miss by the kiln Tuesday — someone walked behind a truck
  that was backing up. Nobody was hurt.
  Two things. Walk around the front, not the back. And if you're the one
  backing, wait until you can see the person, not until you think they
  moved.
  That's it. Bring anything else up with me at shift start.
