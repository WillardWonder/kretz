Wisconsin Statewide Parcel Map · V11 / 2025

Parcel Owner Lookup

Find property owners by county and parcel size, straight from the State Cartographer's Office public parcel layer. No account, no key — public tax-roll records.

Filters on the county's reported acreage (GIS where available, else deeded or assessed). Capped at 5,000 rows — raise the minimum to narrow a big county.

Source: Wisconsin Statewide Parcel Map Initiative (Wisconsin State Cartographer's Office / Dept. of Administration), endpoint Wisconsin_Statewide_Parcels/FeatureServer/0. Owner names reflect the most recent tax roll submitted by each county and may lag recent sales; some counties don't submit every attribute. This is parcel/tax data, not a legal boundary survey. Provided free of charge by the State of Wisconsin.
