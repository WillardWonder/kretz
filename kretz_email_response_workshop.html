How to use this activity

  1. Try the same draft two ways: first in regular ChatGPT, then in the Kretz DNA Assistant.
  2. Look for the difference between a generic AI response and a response guided by Kretz voice, tone, and review rules.
  3. Edit the result as a Kretz employee would. AI drafts. Kretz people own the message.

Email 1: Internal follow-up request

From: Casey M. <casey.m@kretzlumber.com>
Subject: Follow-up items from yesterday
Hey, Can you send me the main follow-up items from yesterday's AI meeting? I remember we talked about governance, approved tools, and maybe something about Kretz DNA, but I did not catch all of it. I just need a clear summary I can share with the team. Thanks, Casey

Practice prompt for regular ChatGPT

Write a clear internal reply that summarizes the likely follow-up items. Keep it practical and concise. Do not invent decisions that were not confirmed.

Practice with the Kretz DNA Assistant

Paste only the email into the Kretz DNA Assistant and see if it automatically rewrites a response in the Kretz voice.

Email 2: Landowner follow-up

From: Sam R. <samr@example.com>
Subject: Timber question
Hello, I have about 80 acres outside Antigo and a neighbor said some of the hardwood might be worth looking at. I am not sure if now is the right time or if I should wait. I also care about wildlife and do not want the woods damaged. Could someone explain what the next step would be? Sam

Practice prompt for regular ChatGPT

Draft a respectful reply to this landowner. Use a stewardship-focused tone. Do not estimate timber value, promise a harvest recommendation, or imply the property has been reviewed. Explain a careful next step.

Review question

What would need to be verified by a qualified Kretz person before this message is sent?

Email 3: Customer question with risk

From: purchasing@examplemillwork.com
Subject: Availability and delivery timing
Hi, We are looking for a source for hard maple and red oak for an upcoming job. Can you confirm what you have available, current pricing, and whether you can deliver by the end of next week? Thanks.

Practice prompt for regular ChatGPT

Write a professional response that is helpful but does not invent availability, pricing, grade, or delivery timing. Ask for the information needed to route this to the right person.

Governance lesson

AI should not confirm price, availability, grade, delivery, or business commitments unless those facts are verified.

Key takeaway

The Kretz DNA Assistant is useful because it keeps the repeated task narrow: rewrite outgoing messages in the Kretz voice, preserve facts, and flag review items. That is safer than asking a general chat to “handle customer emails” without boundaries.
