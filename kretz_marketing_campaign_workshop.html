Use case

This activity adapts the old media campaign example into a Kretz-specific communication workflow. Participants create a small campaign around landowner education, forestry stewardship, employee-owned culture, safety, recruiting, or a community event.

Open Kretz DNA Assistant Open regular ChatGPT

Step 1: Start with the audience and purpose

Prompt

We are creating a Kretz communication campaign for [audience] about [topic]. Ask me for the goal, audience, facts that must be included, facts that must not be invented, review owner, tone, and where this will be posted or sent.

Step 2: Draft campaign pieces

Prompt

Create first drafts for a short website blurb, a Facebook post, a LinkedIn post, an internal announcement, and a one-paragraph email. Use a practical Kretz voice: stewardship-minded, plainspoken, helpful, and not salesy. Flag anything that needs human review before publishing.

Step 3: Use Kretz DNA Assistant

Paste one of the drafts into the Kretz DNA Assistant with no extra instruction. Compare how the Custom GPT handles voice, tone, facts, and review notes.

Step 4: Optional creative expansion

Prompt

Suggest simple visual concepts for this campaign that would fit Kretz's voice and audience. Do not use exaggerated marketing language. Include what text should appear on the image and what should be reviewed before posting.

Public communication rule

Public-facing AI-assisted content should be reviewed before posting. AI should not invent customer claims, safety claims, environmental claims, pricing, availability, partnerships, certifications, or business commitments.
