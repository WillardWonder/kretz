Process: Landowner inquiry intake

Rough notes:
- Landowner calls or emails asking about timber, forest health, property goals, or possible harvest.
- Office or forestry staff collects name, contact info, county, approximate acreage, basic goals, and any urgent concerns.
- Staff should avoid giving timber value or harvest recommendations before qualified review.
- If it seems appropriate, the inquiry is routed to forestry or land staff for follow-up.
- Follow-up may include asking for more property details, scheduling a call, reviewing public information, or arranging a site visit.
- Notes should be saved somewhere consistent so follow-up is not lost.
- Sensitive info includes landowner contact info, property details, valuation discussions, contract terms, and internal strategy.
- AI could help draft follow-up emails and checklists, but a Kretz person must review before sending.
