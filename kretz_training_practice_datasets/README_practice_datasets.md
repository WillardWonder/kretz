# Kretz AI Training Practice Datasets

These files are synthetic practice materials created for the Kretz AI Leadership and Governance training. They are realistic enough for hands-on exercises, but they are not official Kretz records, customer records, landowner records, safety guidance, pricing, timber valuation, or business commitments.

## Files

- landowner_call_notes_synthetic.csv
  Use with the Email Response Workshop, SOP Workshop, Kretz DNA Assistant, and Kretz AI Decision Guide.

- customer_inquiries_synthetic.csv
  Use to practice customer-facing response drafting without inventing pricing, availability, delivery, or commitments.

- crm_followup_sample_synthetic.csv
  Use with the Kretz AI Decision Guide to decide whether a workflow belongs in existing software, CRM, automation, Custom GPT, or process cleanup.

- vendor_research_starter_synthetic.csv
  Use with the Research and Vendor Comparison Workshop.

- ai_meeting_notes_synthetic.txt
  Use to practice summaries, action items, governance risks, and next steps.

- process_notes_landowner_intake_synthetic.txt
  Use with the SOP and Documentation Workshop.

- marketing_campaign_brief_synthetic.txt
  Use with the Marketing and Outreach Campaign Workshop.

- safety_toolbox_notes_synthetic.txt
  Use to discuss safe boundaries for AI-assisted safety content.

- kretz_voice_samples_synthetic.txt
  Use to show how examples can improve tone matching.

## Suggested Upload Activity

1. Download one file.
2. Upload it to regular ChatGPT or paste it into the Kretz AI Decision Guide or Kretz DNA Assistant.
3. Ask for a summary, draft, checklist, risk review, or decision recommendation.
4. Identify what still needs human review.
5. Discuss whether the task should be AI, existing software, automation, process cleanup, vendor system, or no AI for now.
