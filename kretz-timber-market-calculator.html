Turning Kretz knowledge into an interactive tool.

This page is built for the AI leadership training. It demonstrates how a web app can turn company expertise, public-facing educational content, and a repeated decision process into an interactive tool.

The important leadership question is not just “Can AI build this?” It is “What data does it depend on, what could it get wrong, who owns it, and what must a Kretz person review?”

Training disclaimer: This is not a timber appraisal, forestry prescription, purchasing offer, contract estimate, safety recommendation, or financial recommendation. Outputs are illustrative and must be reviewed by qualified Kretz personnel before business use.

How to use this in the demo

1 Load sample data or enter a simple forest profile.

2 Adjust species, volume, distance, market cycle, and growth assumptions.

3 Review the generated valuation, chart, strategy notes, and governance warnings.

4 Ask participants what this tool should never do without human review.

What this tool must not do

This prototype should not be used to make final timber valuations, harvest recommendations, contract terms, payment commitments, tax/legal advice, MFL guidance, safety guidance, or customer/landowner promises.

It should educate, organize assumptions, and support a conversation. It should not replace forestry judgment.

Governance questions for leaders

  • Who owns and maintains the assumptions?
  • What data can be entered safely?
  • Which outputs require forester review?
  • Could a landowner mistake this for an offer or appraisal?
  • Should this be internal only, customer-facing, or not used at all?
  • What source materials should be approved before a real version is built?
